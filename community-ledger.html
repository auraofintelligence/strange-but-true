The new spine

AUD purchase first. Crypto Public Ledger afterwards.

This page is not asking people to buy crypto before they understand the story. It uses the normal download shop as the front door. A player buys a qualifying bundle in AUD, receives their files through a private link and temporary password, then gets invited to start a public profile if they want to enter the ledger game.

Unlock paths

Different purchases unlock different branches.

The branches are not random. Music unlocks the iCi/SOL story branch after the player has listened through at least once. Writing and supporter purchases unlock the deeper public-profile branch connected to XRP, civic evidence and future C-Hour logic.

iCi / SOLMusic branch

Unlocked by purchasing at least the $50 Full Discography Pack or equivalent qualifying music bundle in AUD, then listening through completely at least once.

XRPPublic profile branch

Unlocked by purchasing at least the $100 Paid Writings Bundle, the $200 Supporter Bundle, or an agreed Super Bonus purchase in AUD.

C-HourFuture sandbox

Not live yet. C-Hours remain a future verified contribution unit for public-interest work, separate from ordinary downloads and speculative tokens.

After checkout

What the buyer receives.

Every qualifying purchase is still a normal digital product sale first. The buyer gets the downloads they paid for, then a gentle bridge into the public game if they want to keep going.

01Private download link

A link to the new download website for the purchased bundle.

02Temporary password

A temporary password or access code so the buyer can collect their files without making the public profile first.

03Profile invitation

A separate invitation to start a public profile for the Community Ledger game.

04Unlock record

The profile can show which branch the player unlocked: music/iCi/SOL or writings/supporter/XRP.

05Future quests

Later, profiles can connect to public builds, grant quests, civic software, Sample Worlds or community contribution trails.

06Choice remains voluntary

Buying a download does not force public participation. The game path is an invitation after the purchase.

Why tokens enter the story

The token is not the product. The trail is the point.

The product is music, writing, creative work and access to the Strange but True catalogue. The public game comes after that: profiles, visible support, quests, contribution trails and future civic evidence.

The iCi/SOL branch is the story and signal layer for people who enter through music. The XRP branch is the public-profile and transaction-trail layer for people entering through paid writings, supporter bundles or Super Bonus purchases.

Wallets come later

A wallet is for public identity and ledger proof, not the first sale.

The buyer does not need a wallet to buy the download bundle. Wallet setup belongs in the optional onboarding after the qualifying AUD purchase, when the player chooses whether to create a public profile and connect to iCi/SOL or XRP story rails.

1. Buy in AUD

  1. Use the normal checkout for the music, writing, supporter or Super Bonus purchase.
  2. Receive the download link and temporary password.
  3. Enjoy the product first.

2. Unlock the branch

  1. Music buyers unlock the iCi/SOL branch after a complete listen-through.
  2. Paid writing, supporter and Super Bonus buyers unlock the XRP public-profile branch.
  3. The branch is a game and reputation layer, not investment advice.

3. Start public profile

  1. Choose a public name or organisation profile.
  2. Connect the qualifying purchase.
  3. Wallet setup can be introduced only when the player is ready for public ledger participation.

Coming next: private download website, temporary password delivery, public profile starter form, and separate wallet tutorials for the iCi/SOL and XRP branches.

The purpose is reputation building in the reciprocity economy: visible participation, public contribution trails and evidence for future civic systems.
