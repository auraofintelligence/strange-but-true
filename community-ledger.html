Public support records

Wallets and dashboards are for visible project support.

Crypto is not required for ordinary downloads or normal local service work. The wallet path is for people who choose to show visible support for a specific project.

A public profile can connect a supporter, a wallet address, the service paid for, the project being supported, and the amount paid. Those records can feed a public Community Honour Board dashboard.

Ordinary buyers

  1. No wallet is needed to buy downloads.
  2. No public profile is required.
  3. You can stay completely private.

Public supporters

  1. Choose a local project.
  2. Hire Luke for named work.
  3. Connect payment, wallet and project support records.

Honour board

  1. Show who supported what.
  2. Show the service paid for.
  3. Feed a public dashboard for community contribution trails.

When the honour board begins

The public ledger starts with the first real supporter.

The Community Honour Board is not meant to launch as an empty gimmick. It begins when someone sees a local project, understands why it matters, and chooses to support its further development in public.

That support may be tied to a paid service: business planning, grant writing, AI agent flow design, project mapping, stakeholder notes, web content, prototype work, or another practical next step.

What profile.md is

A plain text public profile you can keep.

The Profile Builder creates a file called profile.md. It is the basic public information that can sit beside a supporter on the Community Honour Board: who they are, where they are connected, what they can offer, what they care about, and what future they would like to help build.

A crypto public wallet is only needed when someone wants a public record of money moving: paying someone to work on the community, or being paid to do community project work. Volunteer support can join the Honour Board without a wallet.

You only write what you are comfortable sharing.

PlaceWhere you connect

Township, roots, local connection, and public keywords.

CareHow you contribute

Skills, time, support, stewardship, groups, and useful local knowledge.

FutureWhat you would build

Joyful, responsible, and abundant ideas for island and community life.

What aura.md is

A deeper private file for useful AI help.

The Aura Builder creates aura.md or separate notes. This is the deeper layer: personal context, AI preferences, devices, world-building, planning, events, music, gamification, resilience, and future tools.

Think of profile.md as the public doorway, and aura.md as the private workshop behind it.

This is also where the future C-Hour and AI transition work belongs. The C-Hour is intended as a non-speculative record of verified community contribution that helps mobilise the care economy: volunteering, mentoring, disaster recovery, elder support, ecological repair, training and other useful work that keeps society functioning as AI disrupts paid work. Luke is already following legal pathways toward a Redlands trial and a possible national carve-out for wider expansion. P4A is where that local ledger idea gets tested as a bigger civic imagination, not as a demand that every visitor become political.
