Events & Media Pricing

Event support is quoted by setup, time, equipment and weather risk.

Small local event and media jobs often sit between $150 and $450+, depending on what needs to be brought, tested, packed down and safely managed. Larger funded, mainland or corporate-style events are quoted at professional value.

Final quotes depend on venue access, power, travel, event length, setup time, equipment requirements and the weather plan.

Community-friendly pricing may be offered where the event is respectful, practical and genuinely useful. Favours, mates rates and Elder-friendly discounts are discretionary. They depend on goodwill, capacity and community value, not on age alone, status, referrals, or knowing someone connected to Strange but True.
