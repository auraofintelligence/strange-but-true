Community project pathway

Start with practical help. Go deeper only if the work speaks to you.

Most people will meet Strange but True through the market stall, tech help, event support, AI training, social setup, music, or a plain local conversation. If that saves you time, clears confusion, or helps a project move, you can pay for Luke's time like any normal local service.

If you later explore the music, writing bundles, or Sample Worlds and see a local project you want to support, the Community Ledger can become a public support path. That is where a public profile, wallet record, and Community Honour Board may eventually show who supported what, what service was paid for, and which community idea moved forward.
