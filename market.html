Market Stall

A visible, friendly place for questions, music, writing and useful conversations.

The stall is a low-pressure way to meet, ask, browse and get a feel for what Strange but True can help with.

At the stall

  • Ask about tech, AI, forms and device help
  • Browse creative products, music previews and download samples
  • Talk through event, projection or outdoor cinema ideas
  • Pick up a card, flyer or QR code for later

Market payments

Cash, PayID and bank transfer are welcome at the stall. These options help avoid extra payment fees, but digital downloads and service bookings are fulfilled after payment is confirmed or cleared.

If you need download access quickly, use the automated card or wallet checkout from the downloads page.
